What Does the Brainstem Do?

The Brainstem is located at the base of the brain, near the spine. Because of its location, it has quick and easy access to the result of the body and therefore is the perfect for controlling involuntary responses. These include things such as controlling our heartbeat, breathing and response to injury (ex. pulling your hand away from a hot surface - quick response insures minimal damage).
