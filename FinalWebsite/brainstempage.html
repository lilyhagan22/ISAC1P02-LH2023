What Does the Brainstem Do?

The brainstem is located at the base of the brain, near the spine. Because of its location, it has quick and easy access to the rest of the body and therefore, is perfect for controlling involuntary responses. These include things such as regulating our heartbeat, breathing and response to injury (ex. pulling your hand away from a hot surface - quick response insures minimal damage).
